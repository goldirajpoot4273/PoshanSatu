Frequently Asked Questions

Common Nutrition Questions

How do nutritional needs differ between boys and girls? ›
Boys and girls have different growth trajectories and metabolic rates, which affect their nutritional requirements. Boys may need more calories as they grow older, whereas girls may have slightly higher iron needs during adolescence due to menstruation.
What are the best foods for a child's brain development? ›
Foods rich in omega-3 fatty acids, iron, and antioxidants are crucial for brain development. These include fatty fish, leafy greens, berries, and nuts, which help to support cognitive function and mental performance.
How can I ensure my child gets enough nutrients as a picky eater? ›
Introducing a variety of foods early, involving children in meal preparation, and making meals fun can help. Offering small, consistent portions of healthy food without pressure can gradually expand their palate.
